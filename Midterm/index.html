Our Mission

Welcome to Team Long Nose! Our mission is to create a website that informs the users about all they would ever need to know about ‘memes’. We want our website to contain a timeline of the history of the funniest content and areas on the internet alongside various information for each individual piece.

The two of us met and found a very large overlapping interest in the internet, but more specifically, in the joy it can provide. Memes are the epitome of this said joy on the internet. So, the idea of sharing memes to the people of the world came into fruition, setting us off on our journey to make it happen. Combined with the power of our love of memes and our goofy personalities, we are determined to create the best version of our website that there possibly can be.
